- **Score:** 59
- **Categories:** cs.RO
- **Abstract:** Humanoid robots operating in human-centered environments (e.g., homes, hospitals, and offices) must mitigate foot--ground impact transients, as impact-induced vibration and noise degrade user experience and repeated impacts accelerate hardware wear.
