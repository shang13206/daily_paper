### 1. Unleashing the Agility of Wheeled-Legged Robots for High-Dynamic Reflexive Obstacle Evasion
- **Score:** 78
- **Categories:** cs.RO
- **Abstract:** Wheeled-legged robots combine the energy efficiency of wheeled locomotion with the terrain adaptability of legged systems, making them promising platforms for agile mobility in complex and dynamic environments. ### 2. Event-based SLAM Benchmark for High-Speed Maneuvers
- **Score:** 67
- **Categories:** cs.RO
- **Abstract:** Event-based cameras are bio-inspired sensors with pixels that independently and asynchronously respond to brightness changes at microsecond resolution, offering the potential to handle visual tasks in high-speed maneuvering scenarios. Existing event-based approaches, although successful in mitigating motion blur caused by high-speed maneuvers, suffer from many limitations.
- **AI 点评:** 事件相机SLAM基准测试面向高速机动场景的视觉里程计/视觉惯性里程计，与移动机器人状态估计直接相关，可为足式机器人感知提供参考。

### 3. asRoBallet: Closing the Sim2Real Gap via Friction-Aware Reinforcement Learning for Underactuated Spherical Dynamics
- **Score:** 66
- **Categories:** cs.RO, cs.AI
- **Abstract:** We introduce asRoBallet, to the best of our knowledge, the first successful deployment of reinforcement learning (RL) on a humanoid ballbot hardware.
