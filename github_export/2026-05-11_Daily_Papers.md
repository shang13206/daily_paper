### 5. Sensitivity-Based Robust NMPC for Close-Proximity Offshore Wind Turbine Inspection with a Tilted Multirotor
- **Score:** 50
- **Categories:** cs.RO
- **Venue:** ICRA
- **Abstract:** Close-proximity offshore wind turbine inspection requires strict clearance control around large cylindrical structures under wind and model mismatch. Nominal Nonlinear Model Predictive Control (NMPC) may violate safety constraints when mass, inertia, thrust effectiveness, drag, or wind conditions differ from nominal assumptions. We propose a sensitivity-based robust NMPC for a tilted multirotor that robustifies the tower-clearance constraint via online constraint tightening.
- **AI 点评:** Matches keywords: model predictive control
